ABOUT US

Our Mission

CityWatch is dedicated to creating safer and smarter cities through community participation. We provide a platform where citizens can easily report common city problems such as damaged roads, broken streetlights, waste buildup, and more. By connecting citizens and local offices, CityWatch helps make small but meaningful improvements to public spaces.

Our Vision

We envision a connected community where residents actively participate in maintaining their city. CityWatch aims to build trust between citizens and local authorities by promoting transparency and quick responses to community concerns.
